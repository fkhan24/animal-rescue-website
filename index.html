WHO WE ARE

OldTown Adoption is a local animal shelter that specializes in feline adoption and rehabilitation. We strive to decrease the population of strays and increase the rate of adoption for our furry feline friends. We welcome all cats into our organization and try our best to aid in any health, behavioural, and re-homing needs.

HOW IT WORKS?

Stop by our Adoption centre or start our online adoption application to see if you’re the perfect fit to our family.
